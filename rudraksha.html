The 5-Mukhi Rudraksha

"The Tears of Shiva that bring peace, health, and protection."

Mythological Significance

According to the Shiv Puran, the Rudraksha tree originated from the teardrops of Lord Shiva. The 5-Mukhi bead represents the form of Shiva known as Kalagni Rudra. It is believed to cleanse the wearer of sins and align their vibration with the cosmic rhythm.

Scientific Perspective

Research suggests that Rudraksha beads possess dielectric and magnetic properties. When worn over the heart, they help regulate the bio-electric current of the body, potentially stabilizing the heartbeat and reducing stress levels (blood pressure).
